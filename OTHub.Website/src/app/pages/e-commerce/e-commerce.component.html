What is OriginTrail?
OriginTrail Explained

OriginTrail is a neutral, open-source protocol enabling trusted data sharing between companies, organizations, and blockchains.

End-to-end information is shared and secured in a fully decentralized knowledge graph network, monitored with AI and oracles, providing valuable insights for businesses and consumers alike.

To learn more please visit https://origintrailexplained.info/
