xDai Bounty

OT Hub is watching for when xDai bounties are processed by OriginTrail ready for you to claim manually.

When your bounty is ready to be claimed, OT Hub will check if you have an empty balance on xDai. If you have 0 xDai you will be sent 0.01 xDai without you needing to do anything!
